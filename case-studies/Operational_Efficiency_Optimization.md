# Case Study: Operational Efficiency Optimization

## Objective
To architect and deliver a Voice AI solution for high-decibel kiosk environments, focusing on reducing interaction latency and increasing STT accuracy.

## Strategic Approach
1. **Defining the Baseline:** Identified that existing solutions suffered from >1s latency and poor child-speech recognition.
2. **Modular Architecture:** Designed an API-first framework enabling rapid prototyping across kiosks, tablets, and mobile.
3. **LLM-Ops Integration:** Implemented Langfuse for observability, cutting debugging time and accelerating production roll-outs.

## Key Outcomes
- **Response Time:** Achieved sub-400ms end-to-end (STT -> LLM -> TTS).
- **STT Accuracy:** Increased to 90%+ in noisy environments through custom DSP pipelines and Pipecat.
- **Delivery Speed:** Reduced deployment cycles from weeks to days using AWS Bedrock and Meta Llama 3.1.
