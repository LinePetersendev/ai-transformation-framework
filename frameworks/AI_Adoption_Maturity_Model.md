# AI Adoption Maturity Model (AAMM)

This model provides a structured framework for organizations to assess their current AI capabilities and define a strategic path toward AI maturity.

## Stage 1: Exploration (Foundational)
*Focus: Internal literacy and use-case identification.*
- **Objectives:** Establish basic AI literacy, identify low-hanging fruit, and define ethical guidelines.
- **Key Metrics:** Number of POCs, employee training hours, infrastructure readiness.

## Stage 2: Integration (Operational)
*Focus: Embedding AI into existing workflows.*
- **Objectives:** Automate repetitive tasks, enhance data pipelines, and pilot business-critical AI solutions.
- **Key Metrics:** Operational efficiency gains, STT/TTS accuracy, reduction in manual effort.

## Stage 3: Expansion (Strategic)
*Focus: Scaling AI across departments for value generation.*
- **Objectives:** Implement enterprise-wide RAG systems, personalized content engines, and predictive analytics.
- **Key Metrics:** Customer reach expansion, revenue attribution, deployment cycle reduction.

## Stage 4: AI-Native (Transformational)
*Focus: AI as the core driver of business strategy and management.*
- **Objectives:** Fully automated evaluation pipelines, AI-driven management practices, and autonomous agent orchestration.
- **Key Metrics:** Delivery speed (from weeks to days), model debugging time reduction, total organizational alignment.
