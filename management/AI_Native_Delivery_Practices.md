# AI-Native Management Practices

Adapting internal management methodologies to align with the unique lifecycle of AI development and delivery.

## 1. Accelerated Iteration Cycles
*Shifting from traditional waterfalls to continuous evaluation.*
- **Automated Evaluation Pipelines:** Reducing model debugging time (target: 40% reduction) through automated STT/TTS and LLM logic testing.
- **Sub-Second Feedback Loops:** Implementing real-time observability (e.g., Langfuse) to track latency and response quality in production.

## 2. Dynamic Resource Allocation
*Aligning talent and infrastructure with real-time project needs.*
- **Cross-Functional Pods:** Integrating Strategy, Engineering, and LLM-Ops into single delivery units.
- **Infrastructure Scaling:** Leveraging AWS-native services (Bedrock, Lambda) to reduce deployment cycles from weeks to days.

## 3. Client Objective Alignment (The North Star)
*Ensuring technical excellence serves business-critical outcomes.*
- **Value-Driven Backlogs:** Prioritizing features based on operational efficiency gains and customer reach rather than just technical novelty.
- **Transparency in AI-Ops:** Providing clients with real-time performance dashboards to build trust in AI-driven decision-making.
